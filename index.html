About us

We are Venetian Blue.

A band from Cork, Ireland that performs in many different venues around the country. We play at any occasion, best known for performing at weddings and private hotel events.

Ger & Steve

Ger started playing guitar at a very young age which was influenced by his elder brothers Sean & Steve who were in turn influenced by the music of Rory Gallagher, Cream and all the great rock artists. Ger & Steve collaborated throughout their teenage years. They quickly developed their own unique style of acoustic guitar playing which led to them playing support to major blues artists Sonny Terry & Brownie McGhee in Connolly Hall. As they toured music venues in Ireland, Steve was building his repertoire of original compositions. In the early 1980’s Ger &a Steve travelled to main land Europe and played music venues in Germany, France, Belgium & Holland. After 2 years they returned home to Cork and continued their musical journey. During the late 1980’s Ger spent a year living & playing music in New York City with various bands and musicians. En-route home Ger stopped off in London for a look around the music scene and ended up staying for 16 years.

Ger & Steve’s connection stayed strong even though they were living in two different countries and they would send each other tapes of musical ideas to work on until they would meet. Ger’s musical journey also brought him to Australia where he toured with an Irish band based in London. Ger developed a love of Jazz and Flamenco guitar and that influence is evident in his guitar style today. Ger moved back to his hometown of Cork and began to collaborate with Steve on his compositions. A brief musical encounter led to them hearing Siobhán singing and playing her guitar. Venetian Blue was formed…..

Siobhan

Siobhán has been singing & playing guitar since she was 7 years old. Her childhood was enriched by music at home and this led to her performing live at a very young age. Siobhán’s passion for a song with a good melody and story has led her to be influenced by artists such as Paul Simon, Carol King and an unending list of singer songwriters. Siobhán loved going to live concerts and regular gigs when she was younger and this inspired her to develop her musical style. Over the years she has played in The Lobby, The Opera House and various music venues in Cork and collaborated with other musicians both recording and performing. This led to a chance encounter with Steve & Ger and she found her musical soul mates. Venetian Blue was formed…….
